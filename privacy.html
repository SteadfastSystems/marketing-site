Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

Privacy-First Commitment: Steadfast Systems builds AI tools with your privacy as the foundation. Your data stays under your control, encrypted, and never used to train third-party AI models.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Steadfast Systems ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our products, including Nexus, our privacy-first AI assistant platform.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address - Used for authentication and account communication
  • Name - Optional display name for your account
  • Password - Stored using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt hashing)
  • Organization affiliation - If you're part of a multi-tenant organization

2.2 Content You Upload

  • Documents - PDFs, Word files, Excel spreadsheets, images, and other files you upload
  • Audio/Video - Meeting recordings you upload for transcription
  • Chat History - Your conversations with the AI assistant
  • Metadata - File names, upload dates, tags, groups, and organizational information

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information - Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage Logs - Actions performed, timestamps, feature usage
  • Error Reports - Crash logs and bug reports (only when you submit them)

2.4 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ We do not track your activity across other websites
  • ❌ We do not sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ We do not use your data to train third-party AI models
  • ❌ We do not share your content with advertising networks

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Service Delivery

  • AI Processing - Your documents and chat queries are processed by our AI models to provide intelligent responses
  • Search & Retrieval - Content is indexed to enable fast search across your knowledge base
  • Transcription - Audio/video files are transcribed using our speech-to-text engine (Whisper)
  • Organization - Tags, groups, and metadata help organize your content

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Bug Fixing - Error logs help us identify and fix technical issues
  • Feature Development - Aggregate usage patterns (not individual data) inform product development
  • Performance Optimization - System metrics help us improve speed and reliability

3.3 Communication

  • Account-related - Password resets, account notifications, security alerts
  • Product Updates - Optional newsletters about new features (you can opt out anytime)

4. How We Store Your Data

4.1 Infrastructure

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure with the following protections:

  • Encryption at Rest - All data is encrypted when stored (AES-256)
  • Encryption in Transit - All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database Security - PostgreSQL with Row-Level Security (RLS) for multi-tenant isolation
  • Access Controls - Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures users only see their own data
  • Regular Backups - Automated daily backups with 30-day retention

4.2 Data Location

Your data is stored in AWS data centers in the United States (US-East-1 region). We do not transfer your data internationally.

4.3 AI Processing

Local AI Inference: Unlike services that send your data to third-party APIs (OpenAI, Google, etc.), our AI models run on our own infrastructure. Your documents and queries never leave our secure servers.

  • LLM (Large Language Model) - Ollama running Qwen models (on-premises)
  • Speech-to-Text - OpenAI Whisper model (self-hosted)
  • Embeddings - Sentence transformers for semantic search (self-hosted)

5. Data Sharing

5.1 Within Your Organization

If you're part of a multi-tenant organization:

  • Documents you share with groups are visible to group members
  • Organization admins can view user lists and invitations
  • Your chat history is private unless you explicitly share documents

5.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may have access to limited data:

  • AWS - Cloud infrastructure (servers, databases, storage)
  • CloudFlare - CDN and DDoS protection (no content access)
  • GitHub - Code hosting and issue tracking (no user data)

We do NOT use third-party analytics, advertising, or AI services.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

  • In response to a valid subpoena or court order
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activity
  • To protect the safety of our users or the public

We will notify you of any such requests unless legally prohibited.

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access & Export

You have the right to:

  • View all data associated with your account
  • Export your documents, chat history, and metadata
  • Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)

6.2 Correction & Deletion

  • Update - You can edit your profile, tags, groups, and documents at any time
  • Delete - You can delete individual documents, chat sessions, or your entire account
  • Data Retention - Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days (backups are overwritten)

6.3 Opt-Out

  • Marketing Emails - Unsubscribe via link in any promotional email
  • Error Reporting - Disable automatic crash reports in Settings

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts - Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts - After 12 months of inactivity, we may send a warning before deletion
  • Deleted Accounts - Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backups - Backup retention: 30 days (then overwritten)
  • Logs - System logs retained for 7-90 days (based on severity)

8. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • ✅ HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • ✅ AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • ✅ bcrypt password hashing (industry-standard)
  • ✅ Multi-factor authentication (MFA) available
  • ✅ Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • ✅ Automated vulnerability scanning
  • ✅ Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • ✅ Row-level security (RLS) for database isolation

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, please note that your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated via email.

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Legal Basis - We process your data based on your consent and contractual necessity
  • Data Controller - Steadfast Systems is the data controller
  • Right to Erasure - You can request complete deletion of your data
  • Data Portability - Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object - Object to processing for direct marketing
  • Automated Decision-Making - Our AI does not make decisions with legal effects

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to Know - Request disclosure of data collected about you
  • Right to Delete - Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out - We do not sell your data, so opt-out is not applicable
  • Non-Discrimination - We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights
